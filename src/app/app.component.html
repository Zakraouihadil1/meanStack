Our Activities

Medical Gym

The Medical Gym brings together joint and muscular maintenance movements, associated with gentle stretching and work on breathing. Back pain can be reduced with medical gymnastics or also called back school. You are preparing for sport or you want to reduce back pain such as lumbago, sciatica ... the techniques of the back school can meet your expectations.

Slimming circuit

The slimming circuit is a fitness class built around cardio and muscle building workshops. Its goal is to burn fat and shape the silhouette through exercises targeting certain parts of the body.




CrossFit

CrossFit is a method of physical conditioning, of the cross training type. CrossFit primarily combines powerlifting, weight lifting, gymnastics and endurance sports.
